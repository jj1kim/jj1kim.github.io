Infra for SNUCSE is a comprehensive infrastructure project designed to support the computing needs of the Seoul National University Computer Science and Engineering department. The project encompasses system architecture design, deployment automation, monitoring solutions, and maintenance workflows. This infrastructure enables seamless collaboration and efficient resource sharing among students and researchers.
