This project involves designing and implementing a custom hardware accelerator for CNN inference. The design focuses on exploiting the parallelism inherent in convolutional operations through specialized processing elements and optimized memory hierarchies. The accelerator demonstrates substantial performance improvements in terms of throughput and energy efficiency compared to traditional CPU and GPU implementations.
